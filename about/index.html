Learn more about me

 

My name is Elaine Dinwiddie.

I am an Adult and Couples therapist specializing in helping people who would love to be set free from psychological stress, relationship issues, and the pressures of life.

Why therapy?

Because I spend my working life helping people achieve meaningful, significant change, and positive core healing.

So I practice one-on-one, and group, telehealth counseling in order to feature the successful progress of people like you toward personal growth in each of their unique journeys.

 

My story

 

Arriving from within a framework that is based on the understanding of how trauma affects people's lives, I can help with stress management, ADHD, PTSD, phobias, Adverse Childhood Experiences (ACEs), trauma, and problematic relationships.

Having a background in the Trauma-Informed Care (TIC) framework, I work with people who want to live happier and healthier lives.

The TIC is an approach to be applied where an individual has a history of trauma.

Additionally, I have received a bachelor's degree in Psychology, a master's in Human Services: Marriage and Family, and a master's in Professional Counseling; from Liberty University, in Lynchburg, VA.

 

My niche

 

I provide compassionate, respectful, and individualized therapy to those who desire to be set free from their past, fear, hopelessness, and despair.

 

My location

 

As a psychotherapist in the Klamath area, State of Oregon, in the USA, I assist you to overcome depression and anxiety at the source of the underlying root cause within your own self through modern telehealth technology and countless late nights and long days of beloved study in neuroscience.

 

My service to you

 

What I offer is telehealth therapeutic psychiatry for adults and couples who wish to maintain a higher quality of life than they historically have done so previously with their lives up to this point and would like to go in a different, nurturing, solid direction.

We will work together to improve your mental health and well-being by teaching you to develop new coping skills to address the challenges you are facing.

Would you like to learn solutions to future obstacles and barriers you may encounter within yourself or between others?

I will aid you to achieve impactful, relevant improvements in your thought life with an emphasis on your inner vitality.

My focus and aim are on using a combination of evidence-based approaches to provide clinical services that target the primary cause of our clients’ concerns.

Therapy can benefit you by reducing or eliminating feelings of emotional worry and distress that may be interfering with your life.

 

How do we get in contact

 

You can call, text, or leave me a voice message and I will be in touch with you.
